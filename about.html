About Park International

Showcasing Korean Excellence in Exports and Exhibitions

Park International Co., Ltd., founded in 2015 and led by CEO Park Chae Woon (Ray Park), specializes in trading, wholesale, and exhibition services. The company exports premium Korean groceries and restaurant goods, partnering with local governments to facilitate exports and providing comprehensive support for smooth customs clearance.

Company History

Our Trade and Exhibition History

Inception and Initial Growth in 2015

Founded on July 15, 2015, by our visionary CEO Park Chae Woon, we specialize in the trading, wholesale, and exhibition of high-quality grocery and fine restaurant supplies.

Our focus was on high-quality grocery and fine restaurant supplies. From the outset, we were dedicated to meeting the needs of our clients with exceptional service and top-tier products, setting the stage for the rapid growth and significant milestones that would follow in subsequent years.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
Surpassing Sales Milestones in 2018

In 2018, Park International celebrated a major achievement as our sales exceeded $600,000. This milestone was a testament to our commitment to excellence and our ability to meet the growing demands of our clients.

Our focus on high-quality products and exceptional service drove business growth, solidifying our reputation as a trusted name in the industry. This year underscored our ongoing dedication to expanding our market presence and delivering outstanding value to customers.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
Strategic Partnerships and Expansion in 2019

This year marked a pivotal point in our journey, as we formed strategic partnerships with local governments, beginning with Gyeongsangnamdo.

These collaborations allowed us to expand our reach and enhance our service offerings. By working closely with local authorities, we could ensure better support for our operations and foster stronger relationships within the community.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
Innovation and Diversification in 2021

In 2021, Park International established a private cargo delivery system, enhancing our logistics capabilities and ensuring faster, more reliable deliveries. This new system enabled us to better control the quality and timing of our shipments, improving customer satisfaction.

Additionally, we diversified our sales structure to reach a broader market. These initiatives streamlined our operations and positioned us to better serve our expanding global clientele.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
Global Expansion and New Ventures in 2022

In 2022, Park International launched our franchise offering authentic Korean side dishes. This expansion reflects our dedication to bringing the flavors of Korea to a global audience. Our operations now span Thailand, Vietnam, Mexico, and the U.S.A., demonstrating our international growth.

To support this expansion, we have implemented a robust document handling system that ensures seamless customs clearance and guarantees the quality and origin of our products.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
Celebrating Culture and Collaboration in 2024

In 2024, Park International partnered with Nonsan City to host the Nonsan Strawberry Festival at Siam Paragon in Bangkok. This vibrant event featured a diverse array of strawberry-based products and cultural performances, drawing massive attention from a global audience

This event underscored our commitment to cultural exchange and collaboration, attracting a global audience and highlighting our role in promoting Korean culture internationally.

Park Chae-woon
Founder and CEO
